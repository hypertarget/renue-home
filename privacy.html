Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026 · Template for review — please have legal counsel review before launch.

Renue Home is an advertising marketplace and matching service. We are not a contractor and do not perform home-improvement work. Information you submit may be shared with independent home-improvement professionals or companies in your area.

1. Information we collect

When you use Renue Home, we may collect: contact information (name, phone number, email, mailing/project address); project details (project type, ZIP code, timeframe, homeowner status, and answers to quiz questions); and technical information (device, browser, IP address, and usage data collected through cookies and similar technologies).

2. How we use information

We use your information to match you with relevant local home-improvement professionals, to operate and improve our service, to communicate with you, to measure marketing performance, and to comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share information

To provide the matching service, we share the information you submit with independent home-improvement professionals or companies (and, where applicable, advertising partners) who may contact you about your project. We may also share information with service providers who support our operations, and as required by law.

4. Your consent to be contacted

By submitting a request, you consent to be contacted by Renue Home and/or its home-improvement partners by phone, text, and email, including by automated technology, as described at the point of submission. Consent is not a condition of purchase, and you may revoke consent at any time. To request information without consenting to automated calls or texts, call the number provided in our footer.

5. Cookies & analytics

We and our partners use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to operate the site, measure conversions, and improve marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your choices & rights

You may opt out of marketing communications, and depending on your state you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. See our CCPA Notice and Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page.

7. Data retention & security

We retain information as needed to provide the service and meet legal requirements, and we use reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@renuehome.com.
