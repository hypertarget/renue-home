Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026 · This is a working template prepared for internal use. Please have qualified legal counsel review and finalize before relying on it.

Renue Home is an advertising marketplace and matching service. We are not a contractor and do not perform home-improvement work. The information you submit may be shared with independent home-improvement professionals or companies in your area so they can contact you about your project.

1. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Renue Home (“Renue Home,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit renuehome.com or any site or page we operate (the “Site”) and use our matching service (the “Service”). By using the Site or Service, you agree to this Policy and our Terms of Service. The Service is intended for users in the United States.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide. When you submit a request, we collect details you enter, such as your name, phone number, email address, project/mailing address and ZIP code, project type, timeframe, homeowner status, and your answers to our questions. If you contact us, we collect the information in your message.

Information collected automatically. When you use the Site, we and our service providers automatically collect technical and usage information, such as IP address, device and browser type, operating system, pages viewed, referring/exit pages, the marketing campaign or link that brought you to us, and timestamps. We use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar technologies to collect this information (see Section 5).

Information from third parties. We may receive information about you from advertising and marketing partners, lead sources, analytics providers, and data-verification or fraud-prevention services (for example, to confirm a phone number or email is valid). If you arrive through a partner, that partner may share the information you gave them with us.

3. How we use information

We use information to: match you with relevant local home-improvement professionals; operate, maintain, and improve the Site and Service; communicate with you about your request; verify, score, and reduce fraudulent or invalid submissions; measure and improve our marketing; personalize content; and comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. How we share information

We share information as follows:

  • Home-improvement professionals and partners. To provide the Service, we share the information you submit with one or more independent professionals or companies (and, in some cases, with other marketplaces or lead networks) so they may contact you about your project.
  • Service providers. With vendors who perform services for us, such as hosting, analytics, communications, call routing, and fraud prevention.
  • Advertising partners. With advertising and measurement partners through cookies and pixels (see Section 5).
  • Corporate and legal. In connection with a merger, financing, or sale of assets; to comply with law or legal process; and to protect the rights, safety, and property of Renue Home, our users, or others.

Because we share the information you submit with marketing partners and home-improvement companies, and use online advertising cookies, some of these activities may be considered a “sale” or “sharing” of personal information under certain state laws. See Section 7 and our Do Not Sell or Share page.

5. Cookies, analytics & advertising technologies

We and our partners use cookies, pixels, SDKs, and similar technologies to operate the Site, remember your inputs, measure performance, and support advertising (including conversion measurement and retargeting through providers such as Google and Meta). You can control cookies through your browser settings. We honor recognized opt-out preference signals, including the Global Privacy Control (GPC), where required. We do not guarantee a response to “Do Not Track” browser signals, which are not yet standardized.

6. Communications & your consent to be contacted

When you submit a request, you authorize Renue Home and its home-improvement partners to contact you by phone, text message (SMS), and email — including through automated technology and prerecorded or artificial voice messages — at the number and email you provide, even if your number is on a state or federal Do-Not-Call list. Consent is not a condition of purchase, message and data rates may apply, message frequency varies, and you may revoke consent at any time. For SMS, reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. You can opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in any message. To request information without consenting to automated calls or texts, use the alternative number provided on our Do Not Sell or Share page.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to: know or access the personal information we hold about you; request correction or deletion; opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information and of targeted advertising; and limit the use of certain sensitive information. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

California residents: please also see our California Privacy Notice (CCPA/CPRA). Residents of other states with privacy laws (for example, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, Oregon, and others) may have similar rights. To exercise any right, email privacy@renuehome.com or use our opt-out form. We will verify your request as required by law; you may use an authorized agent, and you may appeal a decision by replying to our response.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with our legal obligations (including consent and contact records), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements, after which we delete or de-identify it.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s privacy

The Site and Service are intended for adults and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 (or under 16 where applicable). If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Third-party sites

The Site may link to third-party websites and the professionals you are matched with operate independently. We are not responsible for their privacy practices; please review their policies.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date, and material changes may be highlighted on the Site.
