California Privacy Notice (CCPA/CPRA)

Last updated: 2026 · Template for review — please have legal counsel review before launch.

This notice supplements our Privacy Policy and applies to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the CPRA.

Personal information we collect

Identifiers (name, email, phone, address, IP address); commercial/project information (project type, ZIP, timeframe, homeowner status); and internet activity (usage and cookie data).

How we use & disclose it

We use this information to provide our matching service and to operate and market the site. We disclose the information you submit to independent home-improvement professionals or companies and advertising partners so they may contact you about your project. Under the CCPA/CPRA, sharing information with partners for advertising or lead-matching may be considered a “sale” or “sharing” of personal information.

Your rights

California residents have the right to know, access, correct, and delete personal information; to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information; and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

How to exercise your rights

To opt out of the sale/sharing of your personal information, use our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page. To make other requests, email privacy@renuehome.com. We will verify your request as required by law and may use an authorized agent process.
